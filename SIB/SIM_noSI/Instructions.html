Part 1



Before the experiment started everyone drew an envelope which contained either a Yellow or a Blue slip. You have been assigned to the Blue group if you received a Blue slip, and the Yellow group if you received a Yellow slip. There are 3 people in each group. Your group assignment will remain the same throughout the experiment. That is, if you drew a Blue slip, you will be in the Blue group for the rest of the experiment, and if you drew a Yellow slip, you will be in the Yellow group for the rest of the experiment. In Part 1 everyone will be shown 5 pairs of paintings by two artists. You will have 5 minutes to study these paintings on your own. Then everyone will be asked to answer questions about two other paintings. Correct answers are rewarded with 1 additional token that adds to your group account. The group who collects more tokens will win a monetary reward of XX€. The other group will receive no monetary reward. Whether your group achieved more or less points than the other group will be disclosed at the end of this study.

When you are ready, please press “next” to start with the first part.
