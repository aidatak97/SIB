Questionnaire



You will now be asked to participate in a variety of hypothetical games and answer a number of questions.

In these games, as in the rest of this survey, none of the tasks or questions will have right or wrong answers. We are only interested in your personal views and preferences.

In each of these hypothetical games and questions, you will consider different individuals. There will be four different types of individuals:

  • Members of different social groups of yours who live in Germany, such as a neighbor of yours, or a colleague at work or school.
  • Randomly-selected people who live in Germany.
  • Members of different social groups of yours who live anywhere in the world, such as someone who shares your values.
  • Randomly-selected people who live anywhere in the world.
